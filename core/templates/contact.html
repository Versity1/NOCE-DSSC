FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

What is the best way to reach the school? expand_more
The quickest way is to call us directly at 0806 600 1500 during office hours (Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM). You can also reach us via WhatsApp at the same number for quick text-based enquiries.
Can I visit the school without an appointment? expand_more
Yes, parents/guardians are welcome to visit during school hours. However, we recommend calling ahead to ensure the relevant staff member is available to attend to you, especially for admissions or principal meetings.
How do I get directions to the school? expand_more
NOCEN DSSN is located within the Nwafor Orizu College of Education campus in Nsugbe, Anambra State. You can use the map above for GPS directions. The school is accessible from the main campus gate.
I'm having issues checking my result online. Who do I contact? expand_more
For any issues with online result checking or PIN purchase, please contact the ICT/Technical Support at 0806 600 1500 or send us a message through the contact form above with the subject "Result Checking Issue".
How long does it take to get a response to my enquiry? expand_more
Phone calls are answered immediately during office hours. Email and contact form submissions are typically responded to within 24 hours on working days.
