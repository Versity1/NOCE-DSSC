Step by Step

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's place at NOCE DSSN.

1

Obtain Admission Form

Visit the school's administrative office to collect the official admission form. Forms are available during working hours (Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM). A non-refundable form fee applies.

2

Submit Completed Form

Fill in all required sections of the form accurately. Attach the necessary documents (see requirements below) and submit to the admissions office before the deadline closes.

3

Entrance Examination

All applicants are required to sit for an entrance examination covering English Language, Mathematics, and General Knowledge. The exam date and schedule will be communicated upon form submission.

4

Interview & Selection

Shortlisted candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for a brief interview. Final selection is based on exam performance, interview outcome, and available places.

check

Acceptance & Enrolment

Successful candidates will receive an admission letter. Complete the registration by paying the required fees and collecting the school's welcome pack including the school uniform requirements, book list, and academic calendar.

FAQ

Common Questions

What age must my child be for JSS 1? expand_more
Applicants for JSS 1 should generally be between 10 – 12 years of age. However, all applicants must sit for and pass the entrance examination regardless of age.
Is boarding available? expand_more
NOCE DSSN operates as a day school. Students attend from 7:30 AM and close by 3:00 PM on regular school days.
Can I transfer my child mid-term? expand_more
Transfer admissions are handled on a case-by-case basis subject to availability. Contact the admin office with your child's previous school records and testimonial for assessment.
What are the school fees? expand_more
School fees vary by class level and are reviewed each academic session. Please visit the admin office or contact us directly for the most current fee schedule.
Are there scholarships available? expand_more
The school may offer merit-based fee waivers for exceptional students. Enquire at the principal's office for available scholarship or financial aid options.
