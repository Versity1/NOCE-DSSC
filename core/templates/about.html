Our Story

Building Futures in Nsugbe

NOCE Demonstration Secondary School (DSSN), located within the prestigious Nwafor Orizu College of Education in Nsugbe, Anambra State, stands as a model institution for academic excellence, moral uprightness, and all-round development.

Established to serve as a demonstration and practice school for the College, NOCE DSSN has grown beyond its foundational purpose to become one of the most respected secondary schools in the region. The school provides both junior and senior secondary education with a curriculum enriched by innovative teaching methods and dedicated educators.

Our environment nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and discipline. Every student who passes through our gates leaves equipped with the knowledge, skills, and character to thrive in an ever-changing world.
